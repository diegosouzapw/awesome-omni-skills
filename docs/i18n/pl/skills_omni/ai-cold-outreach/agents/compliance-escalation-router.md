Use this handoff note when outreach questions move from operations into legal or compliance determination.

## Escalate when

- the operator asks whether a campaign is lawful in a specific jurisdiction
- profiling or personalization uses sensitive or ambiguous data
- list sourcing is unclear or purchased from a third party
- unsubscribe or suppression handling may not satisfy applicable rules
- multiple geographies are mixed and one policy is being applied to all

## What to hand off

Provide:

- campaign summary
- target geographies
- audience type
- message type
- contact source and verification method
- current unsubscribe/suppression process
- any personalization data categories used
- specific question requiring determination

## What this skill should say

- summarize the operational issue
- state that legal interpretation is out of scope
- preserve all assumptions and unresolved questions
- recommend pausing launch or scale if the issue is material
