## Fast diagnostic prompts

- What are we selling, to whom, and at what deal size?
- Which channel currently creates the highest-quality responses?
- What is the next step after someone engages with content?
- What owned audience or capture path exists today?
- How are we tracking content influence today?

## Common failure modes

- too many channels
- no owned capture path
- weak CTA alignment
- missing attribution hygiene
- newsletter without nurture
- content topics attracting the wrong audience
