Use this checklist before launching a human-recorded, AI-personalized, or demo-led outreach campaign.

## 1. Personal data and personalization scope

- [ ] We can explain what prospect data is being used.
- [ ] We are using only the minimum personal or company data needed for the outreach.
- [ ] We are not using sensitive personal data.
- [ ] We have reviewed whether the personalization approach is allowed under company policy and the target jurisdiction.
- [ ] If lawful basis or consent is unclear, we escalated before launch.

## 2. Identity, likeness, and synthetic media

- [ ] The sender authorized use of their face, voice, or avatar.
- [ ] We are not impersonating another real person, customer, business, or public authority.
- [ ] We are not implying that a materially AI-generated video was individually recorded if that would mislead the recipient.
- [ ] We have a policy for when AI generation or avatar use should be disclosed.
- [ ] We have a fallback human-recorded or text-first path if synthetic media creates trust risk.

## 3. Claims, proof, and endorsements

- [ ] Any testimonial, logo, screenshot, or customer result used in the video is approved.
- [ ] Performance claims are accurate, current, and supportable.
- [ ] We are not using customer proof in a misleading way.
- [ ] Material relationships or endorsement disclosures are handled according to policy.

## 4. Screenshots, demos, and artifacts

- [ ] Screenshots of the prospect's site, profile, or product are necessary for the message.
- [ ] The artifact does not expose confidential or private information.
- [ ] Prospect-specific images and generated assets have a retention and deletion plan.
- [ ] Internal reviewers can verify the artifact before scaling.

## 5. Escalation triggers

Escalate before launch if any of the following are true:

- the outreach targets regulated industries or sensitive buyer segments
- the workflow relies on cloned likeness, voice, or avatar and approval is unclear
- the campaign uses customer proof that has not been approved
- lawful basis, consent, or disclosure obligations are unclear
- the team wants to present synthetic output as if it were individually recorded when that would mislead

## Notes

This checklist supports safer operations. It does not replace legal review or jurisdiction-specific compliance advice.
