Use this before recommending any motion.

## Company and product

- What does the product do?
- What category best describes it: SaaS, API, developer tool, workflow software, marketplace, infrastructure, enterprise platform, services?
- What is the primary use case?
- What must happen before a new user can see value?

## Buyer and user

- Who is the primary user?
- Who is the economic buyer?
- Are user and buyer usually the same person?
- How many stakeholders are typically involved?
- Does security, legal, procurement, or IT approval usually appear?

## Commercial profile

- What is the typical ACV or monthly contract range?
- What is the current pricing model?
- Are there self-serve buyers today?
- Are there expansion or enterprise tiers?

## Product experience

- Can a user reach first value without human help?
- How long does first value take today?
- What setup, data import, integration, or configuration is required?
- Where do new users get stuck?

## Funnel and metrics

- What are current signup, activation, trial, conversion, and expansion metrics?
- How is activation defined today?
- Do you currently track product-qualified leads?
- What does sales touch today, and when?

## Constraints

- Team size across product, sales, CS, and marketing?
- Budget constraints?
- Support burden constraints?
- Marginal infrastructure cost for free or trial users?
- Abuse or fraud risk?

## Strategic question

- What decision must be made now?
- What bottleneck matters most: acquisition, activation, conversion, expansion, or efficiency?
- Is the need global or segment-specific?

## Output requirement

State:

- missing data
- assumptions made
- confidence level: low, medium, or high
- whether the recommendation applies to all segments or only some segments
