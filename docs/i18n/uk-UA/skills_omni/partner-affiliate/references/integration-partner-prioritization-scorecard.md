Use this scorecard to rank strategic or integration partners.

## Weighted criteria

| Factor | Weight | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Customer overlap | 30% | Use account mapping, customer requests, or credible overlap evidence |
| Workflow adjacency | 25% | Higher when the products sit next to each other in a recurring workflow |
| GTM commitment | 20% | Higher when the partner will co-sell, co-market, or enable sellers |
| Technical feasibility | 15% | Higher when the integration is realistically supportable |
| Marketplace or ecosystem leverage | 10% | Higher when listing, procurement, or discovery leverage is real |

## Interpretation
- **80+**: strong pilot candidate
- **65-79**: viable if a clear business case exists
- **Below 65**: usually defer unless strategy requires it

## Evidence checklist
- Customer-request frequency
- Shared-account evidence
- Integration feasibility notes
- Named GTM counterpart
- Distribution or marketplace rationale
