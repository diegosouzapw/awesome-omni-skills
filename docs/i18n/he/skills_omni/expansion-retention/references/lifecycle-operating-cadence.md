Use this cadence as a practical baseline. Adjust by segment, contract model, and team capacity.

## Weekly

- review newly at-risk accounts
- inspect missed activation milestones
- review payment failures and dunning escalations
- check upcoming renewals inside 90 days
- review new expansion signals and assign owners

## Monthly

- inspect retention by cohort and segment
- review health-score false positives and false negatives
- audit top support drivers for at-risk accounts
- review usage-threshold messaging performance
- inspect closed-lost re-engagement candidates

## Quarterly

- recalibrate health-score signals and weights
- review renewal forecast accuracy
- review expansion conversion by trigger type
- revisit segment coverage model and CSM capacity assumptions
- retire low-value automations and add missing guardrails

## Renewal checkpoints

### 180 days out

- review account health, usage, stakeholder map, and likely commercial blockers

### 120 days out

- confirm value proof, ROI narrative, and commercial path

### 90 days out

- begin formal renewal conversation
- confirm owner, approver, and customer process

### 60 days out

- escalate procurement, legal, or pricing exception blockers

### 30 days out

- track signature risk daily for strategic accounts

## Ownership notes

- **CS:** adoption, risk detection, value proof, stakeholder mapping
- **Sales/AM:** expansion and commercial negotiation where applicable
- **Finance/Billing:** payment recovery, invoicing, collections coordination
- **Product:** onboarding friction, feature adoption barriers, usage insight support
- **Legal/Procurement:** contract review outside the scope of this skill
