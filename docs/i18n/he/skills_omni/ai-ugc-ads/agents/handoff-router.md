Use this router when the task drifts outside the center of gravity of this skill.

## Escalate or hand off when

### Legal or compliance review is required

Examples:
- regulated claims
- final endorsement review
- contract terms
- consent or likeness disputes

Action: stop short of legal advice and route to appropriate human counsel or compliance workflow.

### Media-buying or account implementation becomes primary

Examples:
- Ads Manager setup steps beyond planning
- campaign build execution
- asset permissions inside live ad accounts
- account troubleshooting and suspensions

Action: route to paid-media operations or platform specialist support.

### Measurement or attribution becomes primary

Examples:
- incrementality questions
- attribution disputes
- dashboard design
- instrumentation gaps

Action: route to analytics or measurement workflow support.

### Influencer management or contracting becomes primary

Examples:
- rate negotiation
- contracting details
- payment operations
- roster management

Action: route to creator-management or operations support.
