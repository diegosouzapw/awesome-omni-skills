Use these as starting points. Keep them brief and ensure suppression happens in the actual systems.

## 1. Unsubscribe / opt-out

> Understood — I'll remove you from future outreach.
>
> Thank you for the note.

## 2. Not interested

> Thanks for the reply.
>
> I won't follow up further.

## 3. Wrong person

> Thanks for letting me know.
>
> I will update our records and avoid further outreach to you on this topic.

## 4. Timing objection

> Thanks — understood.
>
> If helpful, I can circle back at a better time. If not, I can also close this out and remove you from outreach.

## 5. Referral offered

> Thank you — appreciated.
>
> If you're comfortable, please point me to the best contact. Otherwise, I'm happy to reach out only if there's a better public path.

## Notes

- do not argue with objections
- do not continue automated follow-up after an unsubscribe or direct marketing objection
- ensure the removal is reflected in all relevant tools
