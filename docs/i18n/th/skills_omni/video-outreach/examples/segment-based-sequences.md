## SMB sequence

- Day 1: Short human-recorded or lightly personalized video
- Day 3: Text-only follow-up with one takeaway
- Day 6: Demo link or proof point
- Day 10: Breakup touch

Use when speed matters more than deep customization.

## Mid-market sequence

- Day 1: "Made this for you" video with useful artifact
- Day 3: Text-only email referencing the artifact
- Day 5: LinkedIn touch
- Day 8: Async demo link tailored to role
- Day 12: Case study or benchmark note
- Day 15: Breakup video

Use when personalization and business relevance both matter.

## Enterprise sequence

- Day 1: Highly tailored human-recorded video
- Day 2 or 3: Written follow-up with specific business case angle
- Day 5: Executive or champion-oriented angle
- Day 8: Guided demo or recap asset
- Day 12: Internal-forwardable summary
- Day 15+: Close-the-loop note

Use when trust, internal forwarding, and deal complexity matter more than send volume.
