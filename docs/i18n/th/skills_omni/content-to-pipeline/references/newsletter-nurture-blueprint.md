Use this blueprint when the newsletter is the owned conversion path.

## 1. Capture offer

Choose one clear reason to subscribe:

- ongoing market insight
- operating playbooks
- templates or frameworks
- case studies
- product education tied to a real buying problem

## 2. Welcome sequence

Recommended sequence:

1. welcome and expectation-setting
2. best-of content or strongest proof asset
3. problem framing and common mistakes
4. case study, framework, or offer bridge
5. explicit next step such as reply, demo, or resource request

## 3. Segmentation ideas

Segment by:

- role or function
- use case
- topic interest
- intent level
- existing customer vs. prospect

## 4. Conversion paths

| Subscriber state | Best next step |
| --- | --- |
| curious but early | educational issue or framework |
| problem-aware | case study or practical guide |
| evaluating | comparison, ROI note, or call invitation |
| high intent | direct meeting or product conversation |

## 5. Review questions

- Which signup sources create the best subscribers?
- Which welcome email gets the most replies or clicks?
- Which segments create the most meetings or opportunities?
- Which topics attract readers but not buyers?
