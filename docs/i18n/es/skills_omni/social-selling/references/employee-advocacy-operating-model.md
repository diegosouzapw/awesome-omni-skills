Use this model when social selling involves multiple employees, leaders, or founders.

## Choose the motion

### Founder-led

Best when the company is early-stage or strongly founder-driven.

### Executive-led

Best when category authority or market education depends on leadership voices.

### Team-led

Best when multiple sales or subject-matter experts can speak credibly to distinct audiences.

## Roles

- **Program owner:** sets goals, review process, and scorecard
- **Spokesperson:** publishes or comments from their own credible perspective
- **Editor or enablement lead:** drafts, reviews, and maintains consistency
- **Sales owner:** routes conversations into follow-up and CRM
- **Escalation owner:** handles sensitive claims, negative replies, or policy questions

## Workflow

1. Source ideas from customer questions, trigger events, objections, and wins.
2. Draft content in the spokesperson’s real voice.
3. Review for clarity, claims, and CTA.
4. Publish from the appropriate person, not a generic brand voice.
5. Prompt employees to add original commentary rather than copy-paste reposts.
6. Monitor replies and route qualified interest quickly.
7. Log meaningful outcomes in CRM and review weekly.

## Enablement rules

- provide talking points, not mandatory scripts
- encourage original commentary
- avoid claims employees cannot defend
- define who answers comments and DMs
- define when to escalate legal, PR, or policy-sensitive issues

## Scorecard ideas

Track:

- active participants
- posts published
- meaningful comments by team members
- conversations started
- meetings influenced
- pipeline influenced
- response time to qualified replies
