Use this skill for paid creative strategy, packaging, testing, fatigue management, and creative diagnostics.

Route away when the task is primarily:

- tracking implementation or analytics plumbing
- landing-page development or CRO implementation
- legal/compliance interpretation
- account access, billing, or policy appeal execution
- platform bidding, feed, or technical setup unrelated to creative

## Suggested routing language

- "This appears to be a tracking or attribution implementation problem rather than a creative-planning problem."
- "This requires legal or compliance review rather than creative workflow guidance."
- "This issue looks like platform configuration or feed setup, not creative packaging."
