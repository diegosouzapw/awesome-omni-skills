Use this note when the request moves beyond partner-program design.

## Hand off to pricing or monetization work when
- The main question becomes packaging, margin architecture, CAC/payback, or commercial model design beyond partner-specific incentives.

## Hand off to outbound or SDR work when
- The operator needs partner recruitment sequences, outbound prospecting systems, or co-sell outreach execution.

## Hand off to CRM ops when
- The next step is field creation, workflow automation, campaign influence setup, or attribution implementation.

## Hand off to legal or finance review when
- The work requires contract language, tax treatment, disclosure wording approval, antitrust review, or payout compliance decisions.

## Hand off to technical integration work when
- The task becomes API design, partner integration build-out, sandbox setup, authentication, or marketplace technical onboarding.

## Router rule
Keep this skill responsible for model choice, economics, governance, attribution definitions, recruitment logic, and performance review. Do not let it pretend to complete dependent work owned by specialist functions.
