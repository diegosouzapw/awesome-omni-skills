## Situation

A formerly strong paid social concept has declining CTR and rising CPA across Meta Reels and TikTok over the last 7 days.

## Working Diagnosis

- Creative fatigue is likely.
- Audience and offer remain unchanged.
- Comments suggest repeat exposure.
- No major tracking changes are known.

## Refresh Actions

1. Keep the core value proposition.
2. Replace the opening hook with 3 new variants.
3. Test one creator-style version and one product-demo version.
4. Shorten the first scene and improve on-screen text clarity.
5. Keep CTA consistent for the first refresh cycle.

## Do Not Change Yet

- Offer
- Landing page
- Primary audience structure

## Review Plan

- Review after enough impressions for directional comparison.
- Compare against prior winner on CTR, CPA trend, and comments.
- If engagement improves but conversions do not, investigate landing-page or offer issues next.
