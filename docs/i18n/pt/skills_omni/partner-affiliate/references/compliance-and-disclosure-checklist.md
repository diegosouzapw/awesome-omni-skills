Use this checklist to identify policy work that must be completed before scale. This is not legal advice.

## Disclosure
- Have material relationships been disclosed clearly for affiliate or creator promotions?
- Are partners instructed where and how disclosures must appear?
- Are channel-specific disclosure expectations documented for social, video, and content partners?

## Claims and messaging
- Are partners prohibited from making unsupported performance claims?
- Are testimonial or endorsement rules documented?
- Is there an approval path for sensitive claims?

## Traffic and acquisition quality
- Are prohibited traffic sources documented?
- Are deceptive SEO, fake reviews, spam, or scaled low-value content prohibited?
- Is coupon/deal-site behavior explicitly allowed, limited, or prohibited?

## Brand and paid search
- Are brand assets governed by usage rules?
- Is trademark bidding policy documented?
- Are restricted terms or brand impersonation rules clear?

## Commercial controls
- Is the payout hold period documented?
- Are reversal and clawback conditions documented?
- Is fraud review assigned to an owner?
