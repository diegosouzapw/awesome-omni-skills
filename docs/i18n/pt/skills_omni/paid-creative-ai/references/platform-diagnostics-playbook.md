Use this file when performance is weak or confusing.

## 1. Policy / Review Failure

Check:

- rejection reason text
- ad claim vs landing-page claim
- manipulated-media risk
- sensitive-category restrictions

Next actions:

- revise the asset and claim
- align the landing page
- remove unsupported visuals or statements

## 2. Weak Asset Coverage or Strength

Check:

- missing asset types
- weak variety within a theme
- overreliance on generated assets
- inconsistent branding or messaging

Next actions:

- complete the asset set
- add varied but coherent assets
- improve theme separation

## 3. Delivery but Low Engagement

Check:

- first-second hook
- text legibility
- CTA clarity
- native feel for the platform
- comments and qualitative sentiment

Next actions:

- isolate the hook
- simplify the message
- rebuild for platform-native norms

## 4. Good Engagement but Poor Conversion

Check:

- landing-page message match
- offer clarity
- conversion event quality
- audience quality

Next actions:

- keep the creative if top-funnel signals are strong
- route landing-page or offer issues separately

## 5. Attribution Disagreement

Check:

- attribution windows
- view/click settings
- event definitions
- brand effects
- reporting lag

Next actions:

- align the reporting basis
- present findings as directional until resolved

## 6. Fatigue or Saturation

Check:

- CTR trend
- CPM trend
- frequency trend
- comment fatigue
- concept age and impression volume

Next actions:

- refresh hooks, formats, or proof angles
- avoid changing every variable at once
- investigate audience and offer saturation too
