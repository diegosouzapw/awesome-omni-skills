Hi [Name] вЂ” we like your content about [topic]. We are looking for short-form creator content for [brand/product]. The deliverable would be [deliverable summary], with [paid usage / organic only] rights and a deadline of [date]. If interested, reply with your rate, examples, and whether you are open to [TikTok Spark Ads / Meta Partnership Ads / raw footage delivery].
