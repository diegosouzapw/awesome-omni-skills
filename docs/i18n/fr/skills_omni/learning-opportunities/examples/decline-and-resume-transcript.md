## Scenario

A refactor was completed, but the user wants speed.

## Transcript

**Agent:** I just finished the refactor. Would you like a 2-minute learning exercise on why I split the service boundary, or should I keep shipping?

**User:** Just keep shipping.

**Agent:** Got it. I'll stay in delivery mode. Short version: the split keeps validation separate from side effects, which makes the next change safer to test. Continuing now.
