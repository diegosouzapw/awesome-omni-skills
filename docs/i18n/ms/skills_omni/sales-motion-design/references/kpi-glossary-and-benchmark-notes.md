## KPI glossary

### Activation
A user reaches a meaningful early outcome, not merely account creation.

### Time to first value (TTFV)
The elapsed time between first interaction and the first recognized value moment.

### PQL
A product-qualified lead combines fit, usage, and buying or expansion signals strongly enough to justify sales action.

### Free-to-paid conversion
The share of free or trial users who become paying customers over a defined window.

### CAC payback
The time required to recover customer acquisition cost from gross profit contribution.

### Expansion
Revenue growth from existing customers through seats, usage, add-ons, or tier upgrades.

## Benchmark notes

Use benchmark numbers carefully.

- Treat external benchmarks as directional, not universal
- Segment by product type, ACV, and motion maturity when possible
- Separate benchmark examples from internal targets
- Do not repeat old benchmark tables as current truth without source and date

## Confidence labels

When citing numbers, label them as one of:

- High confidence: directly sourced and well-scoped
- Medium confidence: sourced but generalized
- Low confidence: practitioner heuristic or imported estimate
