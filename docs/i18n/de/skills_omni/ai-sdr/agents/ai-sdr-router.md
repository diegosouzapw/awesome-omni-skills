Use this note when the task drifts outside the center of gravity of the AI SDR skill.

## Stay in this skill when

- the task is mainly about AI SDR operating design
- the team needs qualification, routing, pilot, handoff, or evaluation guidance
- the work is still at the workflow and governance layer

## Hand off when

### Legal or compliance interpretation dominates

Hand off to legal or compliance review if the team needs:

- jurisdiction-specific interpretation
- direct legal conclusions
- advice on disputed obligations

### Deliverability engineering dominates

Hand off to a deliverability specialist if the team needs:

- DNS implementation work
- mailbox infrastructure debugging
- sender platform configuration troubleshooting

### CRM implementation dominates

Hand off to CRM or RevOps specialists if the team needs:

- object or field implementation
- deduplication logic changes
- assignment-rule or workflow automation changes

### Prompting or eval engineering dominates

Hand off to prompt engineering or eval specialists if the team needs:

- classifier redesign
- prompt architecture work
- regression harness implementation
- formal precision and recall analysis

### Copy strategy dominates

Hand off to cold outreach or messaging specialists if the team needs:

- subject-line iteration
- sequence copywriting
- personalization copy quality review

## Escalation rule

If the operator cannot safely answer without making legal, security, pricing, or contractual claims, move the task behind human review.
