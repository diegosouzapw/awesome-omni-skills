Use this checklist to run a repeatable UGC program.

## Intake and Briefing

- [ ] Review active priorities by objective and channel.
- [ ] Confirm upcoming creator briefs include rights, disclosure, and approval details.
- [ ] Confirm any regulated-category creative has human review assigned.

## Permissions and Asset Readiness

- [ ] Check upcoming Spark Ads or Partnership Ads dependencies.
- [ ] Check authorization windows and creator approvals.
- [ ] Confirm raw footage, captions, thumbnails, and exports are ready.

## Testing and Launch

- [ ] Launch only tests with a written hypothesis and defined variable.
- [ ] Avoid shipping major offer and creative changes simultaneously unless documented.
- [ ] Record campaign IDs or naming conventions consistently.

## Review and Decisions

- [ ] Review leading indicators and business outcomes separately.
- [ ] Kill, refresh, or scale based on evidence, not on single-metric excitement.
- [ ] Record why a creative won or lost.

## Fatigue Prevention

- [ ] Queue new hooks for top concepts.
- [ ] Refresh at least one creator, opening, or visual style for aging winners.
- [ ] Maintain a backlog of new concepts before current winners degrade.

## Admin

- [ ] Update creator payments and delivery status.
- [ ] Update rights expiration dates.
- [ ] Flag any unresolved compliance or permissions issues for escalation.
