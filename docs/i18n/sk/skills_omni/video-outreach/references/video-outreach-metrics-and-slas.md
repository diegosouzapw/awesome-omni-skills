Use this reference to define what success means and what should happen after an engagement signal.

## Core metrics

| Metric | Definition | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sent | Outreach messages containing a video or demo link | Base activity count |
| Delivered | Message reached inbox or channel destination if known | May be incomplete in some tools |
| Opened | Email opened if available | Often noisy due to privacy features |
| Played | Prospect clicked through and started the video | Better than open as an intent signal |
| Watched 50%+ | Prospect viewed at least half the video | Useful for triage, not perfect truth |
| Replied | Prospect responded in any channel | Core outcome metric |
| Booked | Meeting or next step scheduled | Primary conversion metric |
| Influenced opportunity | Outreach materially contributed to pipeline | Requires CRM discipline |

## Recommended minimum CRM fields

- owner
- campaign or sequence name
- target segment
- asset type: human video, AI video, demo, recap
- CTA
- send date
- play status if available
- watch threshold reached if available
- reply date
- meeting booked date

## Engagement-based follow-up SLA

| Signal | Meaning | Recommended action | SLA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| No play | Message seen or ignored; interest unclear | Retry with a better subject line or different channel | 1-3 business days |
| 1-25% watch | Mild curiosity | Send a shorter text follow-up with the main takeaway | Same day or next day |
| 25-50% watch | Some engagement | Send a tighter follow-up or shorter clip | Within 1 business day |
| 50-75% watch | Meaningful interest | Follow up directly with one clear CTA | Same day |
| 75%+ watch | High intent | Prioritize the account and follow up quickly | As soon as practical |
| Replay or internal share | Strong buying signal | Arm the champion with recap, demo, or ROI material | Same day |

## Interpretation cautions

- Open data may be inflated or hidden by privacy protections.
- Security scanners and bots can trigger false events.
- Missing watch data does not always mean low interest.
- Use replies, meetings, and pipeline influence as the final decision metrics.
