## Payable-event design
- What specific event becomes payable?
- Why is this event better than raw signup?
- Is the payout tied to booking, invoicing, collection, retention, or expansion?
- What events trigger reversal or clawback?

## Incentive structure options
- One-time payout:
- Recurring payout for fixed duration:
- Ongoing revenue share:
- Margin / discount structure:
- Tiered performance incentive:

## Guardrail questions
- Would this still make sense if conversion rate fell by 25%?
- Would this still make sense if refund or churn rose materially?
- Are you paying on top-line bookings that never become realized cash?
- Is there a hold period long enough to catch common reversals?
- Are incentives likely to create spam or low-quality partner behavior?
