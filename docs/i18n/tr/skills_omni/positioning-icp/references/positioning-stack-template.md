## 4. Alternative framing

What the buyer would otherwise choose.

Primary alternatives:
- incumbent vendor
- internal workflow or spreadsheet
- in-house build
- doing nothing

For each major alternative, capture:
- why buyers choose it today
- where it is strong
- where your wedge is stronger
- where you are honestly weaker

## Positioning statement

For [target segment] who [situation or trigger], [product] is the [category] that [wedge], unlike [primary alternative], which [limitation in this situation]. We prove this with [proof vector].

## Review questions

- Could a competitor say the same thing?
- Does the statement anchor in a known category?
- Is the wedge tied to an urgent workflow problem?
- Is the proof credible enough for a buyer?
- Is the segment narrow enough to act on?
