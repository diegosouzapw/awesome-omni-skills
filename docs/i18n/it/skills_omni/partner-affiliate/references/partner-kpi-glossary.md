## Core metrics

- **Activation rate:** Percentage of approved partners that complete the first meaningful action within the review window.
- **Time to first qualified referral:** Time from approval to first accepted referral or equivalent success event.
- **Partner-sourced pipeline share:** Portion of pipeline originated by partners under documented rules.
- **Partner-influenced pipeline share:** Portion of pipeline materially advanced by partners but not originated by them.
- **Conversion rate by partner cohort:** Performance comparison by segment, source, tier, or recruitment batch.
- **Payout reversal rate:** Share of provisional payouts later reversed due to refunds, churn, fraud, or policy violations.
- **Partner concentration ratio:** Revenue share controlled by the top 5 or top 10 partners.
- **Retention of partner-sourced customers:** Whether partner-acquired customers persist at acceptable levels.

## Usage notes

Do not compare programs only by partner count. A small active cohort can outperform a large inactive one.

Use both volume and quality metrics. High click volume with poor retention is usually a warning sign, not a success indicator.
