Use this scorecard weekly and monthly.

## Weekly review

| Area | Metric | Question |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Production | pillar assets published | Did we publish the planned pillar? |
| Production | derivatives per pillar | Are we repurposing enough without overproducing? |
| Distribution | posts sent by channel | Did we hit the planned cadence? |
| Engagement | comments, replies, saves, retention | Is the audience responding with real interest? |
| Capture | newsletter signups or lead captures | Did content move people to an owned destination? |
| CTA | clicks, replies, DMs, bookings | Did the CTA create the intended next step? |
| Sales signal | qualified conversations | Are we seeing ICP-aligned responses? |

## Monthly review

| Area | Metric | Question |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pipeline | content-sourced leads | Did content originate measurable demand? |
| Pipeline | content-influenced opportunities | Did content help move active deals? |
| Newsletter | subscriber-to-meeting progression | Is nurture producing revenue movement? |
| Efficiency | pipeline per hour invested | Which channel earns continued effort? |
| Topic fit | topic-to-opportunity correlation | Which themes create qualified demand? |

## Interpretation notes

- Strong engagement with weak capture usually points to CTA or offer problems.
- Strong capture with weak meetings usually points to nurture or audience-quality problems.
- Weak consistency makes all attribution harder to interpret.
- Compare channels by quality of response, not raw reach alone.
