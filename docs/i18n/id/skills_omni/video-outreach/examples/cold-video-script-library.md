Use these as starting points, not final copy.

## 1. First-touch human-recorded cold video

**Structure**
- 0:00-0:05: Name + company + reason
- 0:05-0:20: Show artifact
- 0:20-0:45: 1-2 findings or insights
- 0:45-0:60: Soft CTA

**Example**

"Hey [Name], I was looking at [Company]'s [page/process] and recorded this because I noticed one quick improvement opportunity. I mocked up a short [audit/teardown/example] for you. The main thing I saw is [insight 1], and the second is [insight 2]. If useful, happy to walk through the rest live or just send the full notes over."

## 2. AI-personalized scale intro

"Hi [Name], put together a quick walkthrough for [Company] based on [segment-specific context]. I wanted to show one idea related to [problem]. If it seems relevant, happy to share the longer version."

Keep these especially short and verify dynamic fields.

## 3. Follow-up after partial watch

"Hey [Name], thanks for taking a look. The main takeaway from the video was [single point]. If easier, I can send the short written version here."

## 4. Breakup video

"Hi [Name], wanted to close the loop on the idea I sent over. If the timing is off, no problem at all. If it would help later, I'm happy to resend the short version or a quick recap."

## 5. Post-demo recap video

"Thanks again for the conversation. I recorded this quick recap to summarize the three things you asked about: [topic 1], [topic 2], and [topic 3]. I've shown where each appears in the workflow and what the next step would look like."

## 6. Async demo invitation

"Recorded a short walkthrough of how this would look for a team like yours, focused on [use case]. If it is easier, you can watch the guided demo first and decide whether a live call is even necessary."
