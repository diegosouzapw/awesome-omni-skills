Use this checklist when AI or agents assist sales-related work.

## Approved uses

- Account research
- Lead prioritization support
- Drafting internal notes
- Drafting outbound messages for human review
- Summarizing qualification signals
- Scheduling support

## Human approval required

- Any outbound message sent to prospects
- Qualification decisions that materially change sales treatment
- Account prioritization rules used for scaled outreach
- Claims about customer outcomes, ROI, or product capability
- Escalations involving procurement, legal, or sensitive customer concerns

## Prohibited or high-risk patterns

- Fully autonomous outbound sequencing without review
- Fabricated personalization or invented prospect details
- Over-collection of personal or sensitive data
- Hidden AI usage where policy or customer expectations require disclosure
- Automated claims that the product or agent can do things not verified by the operator

## Logging and auditability

Keep a basic record of:

- what the AI system did
- what data it used
- what human reviewed it
- what was approved or rejected
- what failure mode or escalation occurred

## Escalation triggers

Escalate to a human when:

- data quality is unclear
- outreach touches regulated or sensitive industries
- personalization relies on weak or ambiguous evidence
- a high-value account is involved
- the system proposes unsupported claims

## Output reminder

When recommending AI-assisted sales design, explicitly state:

- the bounded tasks AI may perform
- the required review checkpoint
- the prohibited actions
- the logging expectation
