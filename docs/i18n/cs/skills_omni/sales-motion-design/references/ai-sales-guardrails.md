Use AI in sales as supervised assistance, not unsupervised replacement.

## Safe use cases

- account research
- summarization
- draft personalization for human review
- lead prioritization support
- routing and note preparation
- meeting prep and recap

## High-risk use cases

- autonomous outreach at scale
- autonomous qualification decisions
- autonomous pricing or negotiation
- unreviewed personalization using sensitive data
- unsupported claims about a prospect or account

## Required controls

- human review before external messaging
- clear approval workflow
- privacy-aware data handling
- measurable quality checks
- documented escalation path for uncertain outputs
- ability to audit what the model generated and what was sent

## Review checklist

Before scaling AI-assisted sales, confirm:

- messages are truthful and brand-safe
- personalization is grounded in verified inputs
- outreach volume is compliant with policy and company rules
- the model is not making unsupported qualification claims
- humans remain accountable for customer-facing decisions
