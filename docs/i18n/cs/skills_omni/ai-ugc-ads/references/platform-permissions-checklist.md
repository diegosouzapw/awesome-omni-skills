Use this checklist before advising paid amplification of creator content.

## Universal Preflight

- [ ] Confirm whether the plan is organic-only, standard brand-run ad, TikTok Spark Ads, or Meta Partnership Ads.
- [ ] Confirm the brand has usage rights for the exact asset being discussed.
- [ ] Confirm whether raw footage rights are required separately.
- [ ] Confirm channels, duration, and geographic scope of rights.
- [ ] Confirm whether creator identity is required in the ad.
- [ ] Confirm fallback plan if creator permissions fail.

## TikTok Spark Ads Preflight

- [ ] Creator account is eligible for Spark Ads use.
- [ ] Creator has enabled relevant ad authorization settings.
- [ ] Exact post intended for amplification is identified.
- [ ] Authorization code exists and has not expired.
- [ ] Authorization duration fits the planned campaign window.
- [ ] Brand account and creator authorization are aligned to the same intended post.
- [ ] Organic-only fallback is defined if authorization fails.

## Meta Partnership Ads Preflight

- [ ] Brand and creator understand Partnership Ads are the intended path.
- [ ] Creator-side branded content or partner approval settings are in place.
- [ ] Correct business partner or advertiser identity is approved.
- [ ] Exact post or ad identity path is identified.
- [ ] Intended asset is eligible for the planned placement and format.
- [ ] Fallback brand-run version exists if creator identity cannot be used.

## Stop Conditions

Stop scale recommendations if any of these are unresolved:

- no paid usage rights
- unclear creator approval path
- expired or missing authorization
- unclear disclosure ownership
- regulated claims under review
- synthetic content being presented as real experience
