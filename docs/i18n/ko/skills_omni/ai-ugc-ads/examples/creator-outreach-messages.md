## Direct outreach

Hi [Name] — we like your content about [topic]. We are looking for short-form creator content for [brand/product]. The deliverable would be [deliverable summary], with [paid usage / organic only] rights and a deadline of [date]. If interested, reply with your rate, examples, and whether you are open to [TikTok Spark Ads / Meta Partnership Ads / raw footage delivery].

## Product seeding outreach

Hi [Name] — we think our [product] could be a fit for your audience. Would you be open to receiving product in exchange for a short-form content test? If there is a strong fit, we may expand to a paid collaboration with defined usage rights.

## Follow-up after interest

Thanks — here is the short summary before we send a full brief:

- product:
- deliverables:
- usage rights:
- disclosure expectation:
- deadline:
- budget range:
- approval steps:

Please confirm availability and whether you have worked with paid usage or creator amplification before.
