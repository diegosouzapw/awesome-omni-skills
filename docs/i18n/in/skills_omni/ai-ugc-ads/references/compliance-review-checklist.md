This is a preflight checklist, not legal advice.

## Endorsements and Disclosure

- [ ] Is the content sponsored, incentivized, or otherwise commercially connected to the brand?
- [ ] Is disclosure required for the platform and market?
- [ ] Is the disclosure clear and hard to miss?
- [ ] Is responsibility for disclosure assigned to creator, brand, or both?

## Claims

- [ ] Are all objective claims substantiated?
- [ ] Are any health, beauty, weight-loss, finance, or income claims present?
- [ ] Are testimonials framed truthfully and not as universal outcomes?
- [ ] Are before/after visuals used in a way that could mislead?

## Synthetic or AI Content

- [ ] Is any avatar, cloned voice, or synthetic likeness used?
- [ ] Could a viewer reasonably believe the content shows a real customer experience?
- [ ] If yes, has the script or presentation been revised to avoid deception?
- [ ] Has a human reviewer approved the final representation?

## Platform Policy Risk

- [ ] Does the creative reference sensitive personal attributes?
- [ ] Does it imply unrealistic or guaranteed outcomes?
- [ ] Does it use restricted imagery or policy-sensitive framing?
- [ ] Is the landing experience aligned with the creative claim?

## Escalate When

Escalate for human review if the ad involves:

- medical or health outcomes
- supplements or regulated wellness claims
- financial returns, credit, debt, or investment outcomes
- earnings or business opportunity claims
- unclear consent or likeness rights
