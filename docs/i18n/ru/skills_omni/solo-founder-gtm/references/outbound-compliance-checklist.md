Use this before launching founder-led outbound or AI-assisted outreach.

## Preflight

- Confirm the target geography and likely marketing/privacy rules.
- Decide what lawful basis or consent assumption applies for that context.
- Use truthful sender identity, reply-to address, and subject lines.
- Include a working unsubscribe or opt-out path where required.
- Maintain a suppression list and honor removals promptly.
- Do not make false claims, fake familiarity, or unverifiable personalization.
- Minimize personal data collected and stored about prospects.
- Keep outreach logs so complaints or removals can be handled cleanly.

## Data Handling

- Store only the prospect data needed for segmentation and outreach.
- Avoid pasting unnecessary personal data into prompts.
- Separate public business contact data from customer support or payment data.
- Set retention expectations for stale prospect records.

## AI-Specific Checks

- Review generated messages before send if they are customer-facing.
- Do not let AI invent referrals, mutual contacts, or customer-specific facts.
- Test messaging on a small sample before increasing volume.

## Important Boundary

This checklist is operational guidance, not legal advice. If compliance interpretation is the core question, escalate to a qualified advisor.
