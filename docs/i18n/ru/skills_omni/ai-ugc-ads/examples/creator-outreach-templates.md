## 1. Direct paid UGC outreach

```text
Hi [Name] — I like how you explain [topic/style]. We’re looking for short-form UGC for [brand/product] and think your style could fit.

We’re looking for [number] vertical videos for [platform], focused on [goal]. Paid usage rights would be needed for [duration], and we can share a concise brief with examples.

If you’re interested, reply with:
- your current rates
- whether raw footage is available
- whether paid amplification / partnership ads / Spark Ads are possible
- your usual turnaround time
```

## 2. Product seeding outreach

```text
Hi [Name] — we’d love to send you [product] because your content around [topic] feels aligned with our audience.

If you’re open, we can send product and a short creative brief. No pressure on style — we want natural usage content if it’s a fit.

Please let us know:
- shipping location
- whether you create vertical video content for [platform]
- whether you are open to future paid usage if the content performs well
```

## 3. Follow-up after interest

```text
Thanks — here’s the quick scope:
- brand: [brand]
- product: [product]
- goal: [goal]
- deliverables: [deliverables]
- timeline: [timeline]
- rights needed: [rights]
- amplification path if applicable: [Spark Ads / Meta partnership ads / none]

Before we proceed, please confirm:
- your rate
- whether revisions are included
- whether raw footage is included
- whether paid amplification permissions are available if needed
```

## 4. Follow-up when rights are unclear

```text
Before we finalize, we need to confirm usage rights clearly so there is no confusion later.

Can you confirm:
- how long we may use the content
- whether paid social usage is included
- whether raw footage is included
- whether your account can be used for Spark Ads or partnership ads if we choose that route later
```
