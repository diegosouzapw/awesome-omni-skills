## Scenario

A beauty brand wants creator-led TikTok content for a moisturizer.

## Safer brief elements

- focus on texture, routine, and product experience rather than guaranteed skin outcomes
- avoid unsupported medical or clinical language
- avoid implying universal results
- require clear sponsored disclosure where applicable
- avoid before/after framing unless specifically reviewed and permitted

## Example talking points

- how the product fits into a nightly routine
- packaging, feel, and application
- who may enjoy the format or texture

## Example restricted language to avoid

- "This fixes acne fast"
- "Guaranteed glass skin"
- "Works for everyone in days"

## AI content note

If any synthetic avatar or voice is used, do not frame the script as a real customer testimonial unless that representation is truthful and clearly handled.
